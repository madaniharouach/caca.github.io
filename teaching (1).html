Teaching

Math circles, on both sides of the desk.

AMMOC

At the Arnold & Marsden Mathematical Olympiad Circle I teach about ten students per week online, mostly olympiad preparation. Seven of them qualified for the AIME this year. I'm also a student there, with a directed reading project on differential geometry with Yaashaa Golovanov, and courses in set theory, linear algebra, vector calculus, and analysis I.

Parimaths

Saturday sessions run by students from the ENS Ulm, covering algebraic topology, Galois theory, measure theory, Fourier analysis. Good coffee. Harder than it looks.

Handouts

Notes from a course I took at AMMOC last summer, cleaned up — intermediate algebra at AIME level, on Overleaf.
