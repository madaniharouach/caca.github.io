Activities

Things I do that aren't strictly mathematics — though most of them keep circling back to it.

Perpimaths

A free extrascolaire math circle for kids in Perpignan. Association loi 1901, filed March 2026. No fees, ever. Weekend workshops built around problem-solving, logic games, puzzles, and the kind of math that rewards intuition over memorisation — the stuff I wish I'd had access to at 14. Peer tutoring, short stages during school holidays, outdoor math days. Anyone can join; curiosity is the only requirement.

We've raised €2,000 to cover our first year of workshops. For the next step — sending students to regional and national competitions, building a small library of textbooks, hosting guest lecturers — we're looking for sponsors. If your company, foundation, or local institution supports youth education, please get in touch.
